INTENTION : A WAY TO PEACE

BY - MD MAZIDUL ISLAM

INTRODUCTION

Have you ever felt being suffocated and losing internal peace . Perhaps Not you’ll not feel in this Gen Z world cause the sound of ‘paisa hain to badi badi baat hain’ and ‘tujh main rabb dikta hain ‘ is always what you heard. When you love someone and that person is with you, you’re happy, you sang ‘tujh me Rabb dikh ta hain’ and when that same person leaves you, either you’re sad and calling bitch whom you named Rabb a little ago. You’ve let dictate your environment how you’ll react. If you’re in an environment where people surrounding you likes you, equal to you in standard you’re happy. We’ve started building ourselves with the price of sacrificing ourselves. You’re insecure of many things and let’s imagine for a moment being in an pristine island now guess what you don’t have to worry about your short height , dark skin or asymmetrical face and of nothing . Does that mean we should stop doing everything cause everything we do is a product of urge the environment create . Let’s deep dive into it . Remember human being have flaws so inform me my mistake through contact section .
