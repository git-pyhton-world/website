Bipyramid pattern a bipyramid is two equilateral triangle one in top of another.

print('Remeber--',
      'You should use odd number as a bipyramid has always odd number of lines')
s=1
a=3
b=int(input('Enter the number of lines in the bipyramid you want:'))
n=(b+1)//2
for i in range(0,n):
    for j in range(n,i+1,-1):
        print(end='  ')
    for k in range(0,i+s):
        print('*',end=' ')
    print()
    s+=1
for i in range(n):
    for k in range(i+1):
        print(end='  ')
    for j in range(i,2*n-a):
        print('*',end=' ')
    print()
    a+=1
