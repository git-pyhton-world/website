ğŸ“š DataFrame from Dictionary

Creating a DataFrame using different datatypes inside a dictionary.

df2 = pd.DataFrame({
  'A': 1.,
  'B': pd.Timestamp('20230102'),
  'C': pd.Series(5, index=list(range(4)), dtype='float32'),
  'D': np.array([3] * 4, dtype='int32'),
